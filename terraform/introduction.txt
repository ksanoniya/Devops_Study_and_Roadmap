what is terraform ?
- terraform is open-source iac (infrastructure as code) tool.
- Developed by Mitchell Hashimoto and Armon Dadgar 2012 in Hashicrop company
- In 2017 terraform create open source policy in bussiness linces  
- In February 2025, IBM acquired HashiCorp and its offerings, including Terraform.
